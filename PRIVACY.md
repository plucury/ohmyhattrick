# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Oh My Hattrick is a browser extension that improves the display of Hattrick
player list pages and can export the visible player data to a CSV file.

## Data Collection

Oh My Hattrick does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

The extension runs only on matching Hattrick player list pages and reads the
content already visible in your browser tab so it can build a compact local
view. When you use the CSV export feature, the CSV file is generated locally in
your browser and downloaded to your device.

## Data Transmission

Oh My Hattrick does not send any data to the developer, to external servers, or
to third-party services.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, remote
logging, or telemetry.

## Permissions

Oh My Hattrick uses a content script on Hattrick player list pages under:

`https://*.hattrick.org/Club/Players*`

This access is used only to read and reorganize player information already shown
on those pages and to provide the local CSV export feature.

## Local Storage

Oh My Hattrick does not use browser storage, cookies, IndexedDB, localStorage, or
sync storage.

## Third Parties

Oh My Hattrick does not integrate with third-party services and does not share
data with third parties.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension's behavior changes. Any
material change to data handling will be reflected in this document.
